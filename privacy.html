Privacy Policy

Effective March 2026

The short version

Untangle collects nothing. Zero.

All data you enter — situation names, stakeholder names, what they want, how they feel misunderstood — stays on your device. It is never sent anywhere.

Data we collect

None. Untangle has no servers, no accounts, no analytics, and no third-party SDKs. There is nothing to collect and nowhere to send it.

Data stored on your device

Untangle does not persist any data between sessions. When you close the app, your session is gone. Nothing is written to iCloud, device storage, or any external service.

Third parties

Untangle contains no third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, crash reporters, or tracking libraries of any kind.

Children

Untangle is designed for use in K–12 classrooms. Because we collect no data whatsoever, there are no special provisions needed for users under 13 (COPPA) or under 16 (GDPR).
